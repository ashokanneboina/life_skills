# Grit and Growth Mindset

#### Question 1

Grit is the significant predictor of success. Grit is not accepting failure until you win. Grit is showing up every day with consistency. A growth mindset is the best way for building grit.

#### Question 2 -There are two kinds of mindsets 
* Growth mindset - They believe that skills are built, and they can improve their skills by practicing those skills.
* Fixed mindset - They believe skills are, born and they can't improve by doing something.
