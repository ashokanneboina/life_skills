# Question 1 - What is Deep Work?
Deep work is doing something without distractions, which pushes our cognitive abilities to their limits.

* Make a routine of deep work. 
* Avoid distractions like social media.
* Prefer doing things in a comfortable environment.
* Get a good sleep daily.

# Question 4 - What are the dangers of social media, in brief?
* Social media will reduce our capacity to concentrate because it will fragmentize our attention.
* It will make us addicted to social media.
* It makes us less creative and dependent.
* It increases rates of anxiety, depression, loneliness, and low self-esteem.
